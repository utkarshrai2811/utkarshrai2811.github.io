01 — About

Who I am

I’m a Product Security Engineer based in Bengaluru with deep roots in application security, offensive research, and security automation. My work sits at the intersection of breaking things methodically and building systems that make the next break harder.

At Pluralsight, I own the full AppSec lifecycle — from triaging HackerOne bug bounty submissions and running penetration tests, to integrating SAST/SCA into 100+ microservices and developing AI-powered vulnerability triage tooling using the Claude API.

What sets my work apart is a bias toward automation. Security at scale doesn’t work when it’s all manual. I’ve built pipelines that save 100+ engineering hours per year, automated recon phases that cost $0.30 per scan, and triage workflows that cut resolution time by 60%.

I’m also exploring the frontier of AI security — assessing prompt injection, data exfiltration vectors, and model abuse in production ML systems, with published research and internal tooling to show for it.
